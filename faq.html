1. How-To Contribute...

The Linux Standard Base's (LSB) written specification is developed by a set of volunteers. Some have more time and/or experience than others; therefore, there is a core team for continuity and stability.

You can contribute to the LSB written specification in one of three ways:

  • Partner with a core member
  • Send patches to the LSB email forums
  • Participate in LSB email forum discussions
As always, your constructive contributions are welcome and appreciated...

2. Packaging

The Linux Standard Base has established a multi-phase packaging taskforce.  The Lowest Common Denominator (LCD) Packaging FAQ can be found in a separate documents.

See the LSB's written specification for more more detailed and finite definitions.
