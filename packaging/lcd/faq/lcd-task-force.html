The task-force

How does the task-force work?

This task-force is just getting started (i.e. in Feb 2001) so our working style is still being determined. We already have a mailing list and a CVS repository to publish our work items in.

Occasionally people may actually gather together physically to work on task-force business. Minutes of those meetings will be published on the mailing list and the web-site.

Where do I subscribe to the mailing list?

At Source Forge's subscriptions page for Lsb-taskforce1 "http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/lsb-taskforce1.

Where do I find archives of the mailing list?

At Source Forge's archives pageshttp://lists.sourceforge.net/archives//lsb-taskforce1/.

Is there a task-force web-site?

Not yet.

For now minutes and such will be published on the Linux Standard Base's web-site; particularly on talks page

Where do I find archives of the work-group meeting minutes?

On the Linux Standard Base's talks page

How to Contribute.

The LSB LCD task-force is set of volunteers that have banded together to get a cross distribution packaging system working.

You can contribute to the LSB LCD standard and implementations in a few ways:

  • Participate in the discussions on the LSB LCD email lists

  • Contribute code and patches for your favourite distributions implementation.

  • Package your favourite bit of software using the tools so we can test this stuff.

  • Cheer us on?
