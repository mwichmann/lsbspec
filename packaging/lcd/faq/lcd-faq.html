Here are some known problems and unfinished work:

Longevity

The longevity of the LCD work-products depend on when the Unified packaging task-force's stuff becomes available. Therefore we have no idea how long the LCD will be needed for. Therefore — even though the first priority is to get something workable out quickly — more than one version may be necessary to provide features missed on the previous passes.

benefits

The benefits of a single LSB based packaging system are so obvious to me (albertd) that its hard to write them all down. The advantages are not limited to the LCD work either. Help?
